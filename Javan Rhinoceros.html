The Javan Rhinoceros also known as the Sunda Rhinoceros 9or lesser one-horned Rhinoceros is a very rare member of the family Rhinocerotidae and one of the five extant rhinoceros. It belongs to the same genus as the Indian rhinoceros. and has similar mosaic, armour-like skin, but at 3.1-3.2m in length and 1.4-1.7 m in the hright, it is smaller. It's horn is usually shorter than 25 cm, and is smaller than those of the other rhino species. Only adult males have horns; females lack them altogether. It is classified under critically endangered.
