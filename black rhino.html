The Black Rhinoceros or hooked-lippes rhinoceros is a species of rhinoceros, native to eastern and southern Africa including Angola, Botswana, Kenya, Malawi, Mozmbique, Namibia, South Africa, Eswatini, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. Although the rhinocerous is referred to as black, it's colours vary from brown to grey. The species overall is classified as critically endangered. Three species have been already declared extinct.
